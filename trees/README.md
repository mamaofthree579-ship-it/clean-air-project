# Trees: Clean Air Project — Urban Forestry Add-On

This folder contains all materials to plan, run, monitor, and maintain community tree-planting efforts that support the Clean Air Project’s air-quality remediation goals.

Contents:
- planning guides, species lists, and site-selection tools
- volunteer outreach templates and sign-up forms
- maintenance schedules and checklists
- monitoring protocols and data templates (air + tree health)
- scripts to estimate carbon sequestration and particulate capture

Use these resources to:
1. Choose appropriate species for your local climate
2. Site and plant trees to maximize pollutant removal and cooling benefits
3. Monitor tree health and changes in local air quality
4. Organize volunteers and record maintenance/planting events
