# Maintenance Schedule (First 3 years)

**Year 0 (Planting day)**
- Proper planting depth, mulch ring installed (not touching trunk)
- Initial deep watering

**Months 1–6**
- Water weekly (or after rainfall < 1") for first 6 months
- Check stakes & ties monthly
- Inspect for pests & damage

**Months 7–12**
- Reduce watering to every 2 weeks if established
- Replenish mulch if dissipated
- Remove weeds competing at base

**Years 1–3**
- Monthly inspections during growing season
- Replace failed trees in Year 2 if mortality > threshold (e.g., 10%)

**General maintenance notes**
- Avoid harmful pruning in first year; remove only broken branches
- Do not pile soil on trunk (heel-in) or use plastic tree guards that cause moisture traps
