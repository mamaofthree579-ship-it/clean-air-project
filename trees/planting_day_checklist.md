# Planting Day Checklist

## 2 weeks before
- Confirm permits & site access
- Order trees & supplies (mulch, stakes, water-bags)
- Recruit volunteers & send confirmations
- Arrange tool pickup & transport

## Day before
- Stage trees in shaded area
- Prepare toolkits (gloves, shovels, tampers, watering cans)
- Print signage & release forms

## On event day
- Registration table & waiver signing
- Orientation & safety talk
- Assign site leads and crews
- Planting rotation: dig, set root ball, backfill, stake, mulch, water
- Document with photos & GPS points

## Post-event
- Record tree IDs, species, GPS coords, planter name
- Schedule first watering visits (weekly, then biweekly)
- Thank volunteers, share photos & short report
