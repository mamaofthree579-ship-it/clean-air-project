# Maintenance Guide — Clean Air Project

Regular maintenance ensures accuracy and long-term reliability of air sensors and DIY air purifiers.

---

# 1️⃣ Sensor Node Maintenance

## Monthly
- Inspect cables for corrosion
- Ensure enclosure vents are not blocked
- Restart device if needed

## Every 3 Months
- Blow dust out of PMS sensor using gentle compressed air
- Verify firmware still connects to Wi-Fi

## Every 6 Months
- Run sensor calibration check using known-values environment
- Update to latest firmware

---

# 2️⃣ DIY Purifier Maintenance

## Filter Replacement
Replace filter every:

| Environment | Replace Every |
|-------------|----------------|
| Clean home  | 4–6 months     |
| Urban area  | 2–3 months     |
| Heavy smoke | 1 month or less|
| Industrial  | Monthly        |

## Safety
- Keep purifier away from curtains  
- Do not operate lying down on its back  
- Ensure fan grills are intact  

---

# 3️⃣ Community Node Maintenance Schedule

Each site receives:

- Automated monthly reminder emails  
- Filter-swap notification (if sensors detect airflow drop)  
- Health report summary:  
  - Airflow trend  
  - PM2.5 stability  
  - Wi-Fi uptime
