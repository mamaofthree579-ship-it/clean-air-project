Community Rollout Playbook
This guide explains how to deploy Clean Air Project devices within neighborhoods, schools, clinics, churches, community centers, shelters, tribal lands, and high-risk industrial corridors.

---

# 1️⃣ Step-By-Step Rollout Plan

## Step 1 — Identify Host Sites
Use this checklist:

- Indoor crowd spaces  
- Schools  
- Senior centers  
- Recreation centers  
- Libraries  
- Churches  
- Shelters  
- Homes near factories or highways  

Record each site in `deployment/sites.csv`.

---

## Step 2 — Deliver Kits
Each location receives:

- 1 sensor node  
- 1–3 DIY purifiers  
- 1 rollout card  
- Setup QR code  
- Maintenance schedule  

---

## Step 3 — Train Local Volunteers
Modules included:

- Air quality basics  
- How to swap filters  
- How to read local dashboard  
- How to talk to neighbors about air health  

---

## Step 4 — Activate Public Dashboard
Each region gets:

- Local map  
- Live PM2.5 levels  
- Rolling 7-day safety index  
- Alerts for unhealthy air  

---

## Step 5 — Monthly Community Touch-Points
Teams send:

- "Air Quality Snapshot"  
- "Hotspot Spotlight"  
- "Action Steps"  
- "Stories from the field"  

---

# 2️⃣ Equity & Inclusion

Deployment priority:

1. **Low-income neighborhoods**  
2. **Communities with industrial burden**  
3. **Minority & immigrant communities**  
4. **Rural areas without monitoring**
