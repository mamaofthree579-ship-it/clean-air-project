# Clean Air Project — Frequently Asked Questions

---

## ❓ What pollutants do the sensors detect?
- PM1.0  
- PM2.5  
- PM10  
- Temperature  
- Humidity  
- VOCs (if VOC sensor installed)

---

## ❓ How accurate are they?
Within ±10–25% depending on conditions.  
Accuracy improves with regular calibration and maintenance.

---

## ❓ How much do they cost?
- Sensor Node: $28–$42  
- DIY Purifier: $17–$30  
- Full community kit: $49–$70  

---

## ❓ Are they open source?
Yes — all firmware, wiring diagrams, schematics, and docs are under MIT License (or your chosen license).

---

## ❓ Are DIY purifiers safe?
Yes, if:
- Filter faces correct direction  
- Fan is not obstructed  
- Device is not tipped over  
- Filter is replaced regularly  

---

## ❓ Does this replace medical advice?
No — it supplements environmental awareness.

---

## ❓ Can this help with environmental justice?
Yes.  
Communities can use:
- Documented PM2.5 trends  
- Time-stamped readings  
- Historical pollution payloads  

For:
